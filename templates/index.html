Honors Thesis in Women's Studies

I'm writing an honors thesis in the department of Women's Studies this year. I'm curious about how individuals choose to label their non-monogamous relationships, how these labels are perceived by others, and how these perceptions are impacted by the subject's gender. The project is titled Alternatives to Monogamy: Self-Labeling, Actual Practice, and What Happens in the Interstices. I have an accompanying website describing the project coming soon!
